About

Twilight follows the story of Bella Swan, a quiet and introspective teenage girl who moves from sunny Arizona to the rainy town of Forks, Washington. Her life changes when she meets Edward Cullen, a mysterious and impossibly beautiful classmate who seems strangely drawn to her—and equally determined to stay away. Bella soon discovers that Edward and his family are vampires who choose to live peacefully among humans. As Bella and Edward fall deeply in love, their forbidden relationship puts her in unexpected danger, especially when a group of hostile vampires arrives in town. The film blends romance, suspense, and supernatural elements as it explores desire, identity, and the risks that come with loving someone from a completely different world.
